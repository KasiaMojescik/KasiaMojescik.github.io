Hi there!
My name is Kasia and I'm a PhD student funded by the Sussex Neuroscience programme, working in the EVENTS lab led by Professor Chris Bird. I'm interested in what allows us to remember events from our lives vividly, as well as how this ability is affected by aging and individual differences. During my PhD, I aim to investigate these questions using neuroimaging and behavioural measures. My current projects include:

  • A behavioural study exploring the age differences in the mechanisms underlying remembering events vividly and confidently
  • A behavioural study investigating in what way does the predictability of events affect the memory for naturalistic narratives across the lifespan
  • Neuroimaging project looking at individual differences in episodic memory networks, in particular in reference to subjective experience of memory
